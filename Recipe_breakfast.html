Overnight Oats

1 serving • 2 minutes prep (refrigerate 4+ hours)
Ingredients
Instructions
  1. Combine: add oats, milk, chia seeds, maple syrup, and salt to a jar.
  2. Mix: Stir thoroughly.
  3. Chill: Put lid on and refrigerate at least 4 hours or overnight.
  4. Serve: Stir and enjoy cold; add more milk if too thick.
