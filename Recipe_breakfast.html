Overnight Oats

1 serving • 2 minutes prep (4+ hr chill)
Ingredients
Instructions
  1. Add oats, milk, chia seeds, maple syrup, and salt into a jar.
  2. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
  4. Stir and enjoy cold (add milk if too thick).
