About Me: Rishit Khinduka

My name is Rishit and I am a senior at Babson College. I spent the initial years of my childhood in Japan and soon shifted back to India for schooling. Although at the time it seemed like a big shift, studying in India has not only allowed me to learn and grasp knowledge properly, but also presented me with the opportunity to stay connected to my roots. Soon came the time to decide and plan my future, and that is when I applied to Babson and got accepted.

Looking back, I chose this university due to its esteemed reputation for being the #1 entrepreneurship school in the world. Hence, I wanted to gain in-depth knowledge of not only how to create a business but also manage existing business like my family business. Given my grand aspirations after graduating this December, I believe the knowledge and the experience will allow me to prosper in fulfilling my future endeavours of starting a Real Estate company, while also growing my family jewelry business.
